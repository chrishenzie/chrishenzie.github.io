software engineer · google

I work on container infrastructure at Google. I try to keep most of that work upstream, in containerd and Kubernetes.

I maintain containerd, and became a security advisor there during the wave of AI-generated vulnerability reports. Most of my week goes to triaging what comes in and patching what holds up.

I co-authored writable cgroups, which lets unprivileged containers manage resources for their child processes. Earlier, in Kubernetes SIG-Storage, I authored the ReadWriteOncePod access mode (a volume restricted to one pod, stable in 1.29). Before Google I worked on Twine, Meta's cluster management system.

My wife and I compete in ballroom dance.

I'm in the Bay Area. Happy to talk containerd, Kubernetes, or container runtimes generally.
